 CHalfedgeGraphThe concept HalfedgeGraph is a refinement of the BGL concept IncidenceGraph and adds the notion of a halfedge: Each edge is associated with two opposite halfedges with source and target vertices swapped. A HalfedgeGraph is undirected and does not allow parallel edges
