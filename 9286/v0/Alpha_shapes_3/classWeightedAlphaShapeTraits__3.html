WeightedAlphaShapeTraits_3 Concept Reference
Reference Manual » Concepts

Definition

The concept WeightedAlphaShapeTraits_3 describes the requirements for the geometric traits class of the underlying regular triangulation of a weighted alpha shape.

Refines
RegularTriangulationTraits_3 if the underlying triangulation of the alpha shape is a regular triangulation
Periodic_3RegularTriangulationTraits_3 if the underlying triangulation of the alpha shape is a periodic regular triangulation
