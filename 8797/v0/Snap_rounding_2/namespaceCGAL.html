ingroup PkgSnapRounding2Ref

Given a range of Polygon_2, compute rounded polygons such that their segments are either equal either disjoint in their interior, as induced by the input polygons. The polygons are intended to be non-intersecting, unless the named parameter compute_intersection is set to true. Any polygon is guarantee to remain a Polygon in the output but may present pinched section or/and common vertices or segments with other polygons.
