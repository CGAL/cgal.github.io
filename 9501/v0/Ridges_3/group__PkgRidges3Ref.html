Reference Manual

Marc Pouget and Frédéric Cazals
Global features related to curvature extrema encode important information used in segmentation, registration, matching and surface analysis. Given pointwise estimations of local differential quantities, this package allows the approximation of differential features on a triangulated surface mesh. Such curvature related features are curves: ridges or crests, and points: umbilics.
Introduced in: CGAL 3.3
Depends on: CGAL and Solvers
