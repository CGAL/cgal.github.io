Template Parameters
ChainComplexType of the underlying chain complex.

The structure contains a pair:

  • L_complex: a pointer over simplicial chain complex (homeomorphic to \(\mathbb B^3\)).
  • K_complex: a sub chain complex mask (encoding the initial mesh inside *L_complex)
