struct CGAL::Integer_snap_rounding_traits_2< InputKernel, ExactKernel, BaseTraits >

The class Integers_snap_rounding_traits_2<InputKernel, ExactKernel, BaseTraits> is a model of the FloatSnapRoundingTraits_2 concept.

It is identical to Double_snap_rounding_traits_2<InputKernel, ExactKernel, BaseTraits>, except that points are rounded to the closest point with integer coordinates.
