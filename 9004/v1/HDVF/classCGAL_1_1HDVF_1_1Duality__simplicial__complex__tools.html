template<typename CoefficientRing>
class CGAL::HDVF::Duality_simplicial_complex_tools< CoefficientRing >

The class Duality_simplicial_complex_tools is dedicated to Alexander duality for 3D surface meshes.

Starting from a simplicial chain complex (encoding a 3D surface mesh), it provides methods to embed the complex into a larger icosphere and generate a 3D constrained Delaunay triangulation.

Technically, starting from a Simplicial_chain_complex _K, the method simplicial_complex_bb() builds a Simplicial_chain_complex L and a Sub_chain_complex_mask K.

  • L : complex built out of _K together with a closing icosphere, meshed by tetgen (constrained Delaunay triangulation)
  • K (Sub_chain_complex_mask) : Sub_chain_complex_mask identifying _K inside L
Template Parameters
CoefficientRinga model of the IntegralDomainWithoutDivision concept providing the ring used to compute homology.

Generates a subcomplex \(K\)K and a complex \(L\) with \(K\subseteq L\) from a simplicial complex _K.

_K is embedded into a larger icosphere and a 3D constrained Delaunay triangulation is generated. Then \(K\), \(L\) and vertices coordinates are extracted and stored in a Complex_duality_data structure.
